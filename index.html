Looking at 131 reviews, reviewers overwhelmingly had a great experience with this company. Customers frequently highlight the swift delivery service, with many items arriving the next day, often at competitive prices. People appreciate the ease of ordering and the good quality of the parts, which often fit perfectly and are significantly cheaper than alternatives. Many consumers found the parts they needed, even for older or specific car models, and were pleased with the exact fit and functionality.

However, some people were dissatisfied with the product quality, describing certain plastic mirror parts as poor and not fitting properly or matching existing trim. There were also instances where incorrect parts were sent, or parts did not fit as expected, leading to issues with returns or exchanges. Additionally, some reviewers experienced problems with delivery, particularly with certain couriers losing packages or delivering them to inappropriate locations.
